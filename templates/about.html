About LoanMax

LoanMax is a web application designed to apply, make and pay for loans from the comfort of your home. My team and i have collaborated well in order to give you a smooth not difficult experience when you need to apply for a loan with our fast and easy application process.

The inspiration for LoanMax stemed up when a friend of mine who works at a finance institute explained to me the difficulties their customers face when appying for a loan, and the errors the encounter when calculating and agents submitting loan requests.
